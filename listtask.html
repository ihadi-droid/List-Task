Top 10 Producers of Rice in the World

As of the most recent data available, the top 10 rice-producing countries in the world are ranked based on their annual production, typically measured in million metric tons. Here's the list:

  1. China – The largest producer of rice, China produces over 200 million metric tons annually.
  2. India – India follows closely with around 160-180 million metric tons.
  3. Indonesia – Producing approximately 50-55 million metric tons.
  4. Bangladesh – With around 35-40 million metric tons of rice production annually.
  5. Vietnam – Produces about 27-30 million metric tons of rice.
  6. Thailand – Known for exporting rice, it produces around 20-25 million metric tons.
  7. Myanmar (Burma) – Its production hovers around 25 million metric tons annually.
  8. Philippines – Produces about 15-20 million metric tons.
  9. Brazil – The largest rice producer in South America, with around 10-12 million metric tons.
  10. Japan – With rice production around 7-8 million metric tons annually.
